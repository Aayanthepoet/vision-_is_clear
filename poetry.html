The Eternal Rose

Beneath the sunlight’s tender hue,
A rose unfurls its crimson view.
Each petal whispers life’s refrain,
A story wrought from joy and pain.

Whispers of the Ocean

The waves, they dance upon the shore,
A symphony, forevermore.
Their cadence speaks of distant lands,
Of timeless love and fleeting sands.
